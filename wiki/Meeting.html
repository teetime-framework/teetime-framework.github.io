TeeTime Meetings

Meetings take place to discuss and distribute ideas (or work) made by all contributing team members. Participants have the opportunity to present their current work or to gain feedback to a problem they encountered.

We strongly encourage to participate actively in the discussion but also like to point out that this will have no further impact in the grading your work. If you want to present something of greater extent, feel free to add it as point on the upcoming agenda and consult your supervisor in advance.

Minutes will be taken and uploaded to this wiki for documentation purposes.
